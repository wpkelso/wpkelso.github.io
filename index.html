Blackbird,

or the work of William Kelso

Just a guy interested in low-level programming, design, and working toward a more sustainable society. Prone to being a massive nerd.

Why Blackbird?

A while ago, I was searching for a new tag to use in online games. I decided to achieve that by manipulating my name into something else. I went from “Will”, to “Willow”, to “Wirlo”, then finally settled on “Mirlo”. Unbeknownst to me at the time, Mirlo was already a word—Blackbird—in Spanish. When it came time to name this site, I decided to just go with the english name, and thus settled on “Blackbird.” Besides, they've a pleasant song.

Why such a basic site?

When I set out to make this site, I wanted to do it in as semantic a way as possible, with the added benefit of wanting to do as little long-term infrastructure maintenence as possible.
