Reading

Books I’ve read and would highly recommend.

    The Death and Life of Great American Cities, Jane Jacobs; Isbn 9780679741954

    Some individual examples aren't as relevant anymore as they may have been when the book was written, but many of the ideas are still extremely relevant. Designing for passive surveillance of the street environment stuck out to me as an important concept that we seem to all but ignore now.
